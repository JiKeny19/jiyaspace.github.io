Project Breif : The project breif was to select a band/artist/genre from the decades _1970_ to _1990_ and create a vinyl record cover for an existing album or set of songs released during this time period with a contemporary design for a modern audience.
I selected the genre of pop. As I was surfing for songs of the following genre, I shortlisted the following songs : Careless Whispers by Wham !, Smooth Operator by Sade, Running up that hill by Kate Bush, Everybody wants to rule the world by Tears for Fears and Last Christmas by Wham !
Concept :The theme of my vinyl cover is retro futurism. I wanted to represent the aspect of traveling back in time to the 80s. The catchy tunes will surely hit people with nostalgia
