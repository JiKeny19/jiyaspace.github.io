Best Serves Cold

Best served cold is a cookbook about cold easy beginner-friendly meals aimed at young adults trying to start thier cooking journey. This book design focuses on typographical approach to a simple concept like cookbook. It follows the theme of a nostalgic pixel game.

Tales Beyond time

Tales beyond time is a glossary telling the various enthralling stories of Myths and Legends from all around the world. This project was a group effort to communicate these stories in a unique format.
