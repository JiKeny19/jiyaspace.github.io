Gallery Wall Display Design

During my internship at the prestigious Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya , one of the premier art and history museums in India, I was tasked with designing gallery wall display for their upcoming Arms and Armor Gallery, they aimed to showcase the profound impact weapons have had on history and society. Thus they wanted a paper collage of newspaper clippings displaying war news as a backdrop for the weapons.
At first I started by making a simple collage. However, I received a feedback stating to incorporate a less structured design. So in each attempt I made iterations and incorporated the feedback provided by my seniors. I incorporated elements like cutouts and using a beige tint to provide a rustic look. After multiple iterations the design was finally approved. The senior curator implementing the idea wanted to make the collage by physically pasting individual sheets so I made the print sheets used in the final case designed.
